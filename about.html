About 02/05

What's MIBI Members Club About?

Our organization is a dynamic international business club focused on bridging leaders from various sectors, with a special emphasis on the export and import trade. Our mission is to connect entrepreneurs globally, fostering trade opportunities and partnerships. As advocates, we engage with government bodies to represent your members' views, seeking collaborative and sustainable outcomes. Through curated events, a platform is offered for knowledge exchange, networking, and forming business alliances. Additionally, financial support options like investments and crowdfunding is provided to our members. Our diverse membership includes professionals from real estate, construction, minerals, metals, consumer products, freight forwarding, agriculture, IT, legal, healthcare, banking, insurance, automotive, along with politicians, diplomats, and government officials, spanning to aerospace. This broad network underlines your commitment to fostering global business growth and cooperation.
