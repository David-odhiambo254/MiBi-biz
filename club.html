MIBI Members Club Constitution

Part I: Preamble

Name and Object of the Club

Name and Location
The name of the Club is MiBi Members Club, operating under Longonot Media Group Limited. Club Address: MIBI Members Club, Kilimani Nairobi, Equity Bank Building, Mobile Number 0789 33 77 48.

Object of the Club

The Club aims to establish, maintain, and conduct a networking and enjoyment hub for its esteemed members.

Constitution

The Club is a proprietary club under Longonot Media Group Limited, a private company owned by its shareholders.

Membership Categories

Membership is by application and invitation only.
